Our Company

We’re a software company with years of experience in technology. We saw that restaurant and salon owners needed an open source point of sale solution that was effective at seamlessly integrating all aspects of their business. Through our expert team of professionals, we designed a software system that will help eateries everywhere thrive.

As a software design and development team, VINACT aims to support those working in the hospitality service through innovations in technology. We know how hard restaurant owners and their employees work, and our hope is that our products make their jobs a little bit easier. VINACT is dedicated to working with integrity and respect, we go to great lengths to have a diverse workplace where all ideas and perspectives are welcomed

Our Skills
